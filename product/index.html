Content Discovery

The fastest way to find and curate a large pool of the most engaging content for your audience.

Flockrush uses artificial intelligence solutions to find and sort content suggestions for your page subjects.

Building big audiences quickly requires great content to be posted frequently – with Flockrush you get access to great user-generated content, and royalty paid images of the best quality.

Having quick and easy access to relevant, high performing content transforms what can otherwise be a long and time-consuming process.

Tell you stories quickly and effectively by curating at scale across all your channels simultaneously.

Curate at scale – the greatest weapon for preparing high volumes of content ready to attract and engage fans.

Content Publishing

Feature rich queues, approval workflows, automated scheduling and blog posting

With Flockrush you can publish large volumes of content across multiple platforms simultaneously.

Unlimited content queues enable you to set up and easily manage large pools of content and achieve huge scale with your content publishing.

Our highly customizable workflow processes and automated scheduling means content can flow efficiently from discovering to social publishing and blog posting but with full control.

UGC Rights Management

Get rights to user-generated content the easy way, with automated workflows to queue publishing on approval

For peace of mind, Flockrush features an automated single-click process to handle approval rights for user-generated content.

With our smart workflow solution, any UGC requiring owner approval can be queued for posting subject to receiving approval.

Once owners give approval content using our single-click process, it is automatically approved for scheduling, leaving an electronic paper trail you can use anytime you may need it.

Audience Engagement

Manage your community interactions with fast prioritization of the most valuable engagement

Engaging with, and responding to your audience is important, but time-consuming, part of growing your audience metrics.

Our customer service tool makes this easy, no matter what scale your approach requires and how big your team is.

Analytics Dashboards

Follow your audience as it grows with powerful analytics dashboards to track your growth and success

Stay on top of all your metrics with powerful social reporting and dashboards that are easy to create and share within teams.

Flockrush features a range of highly customizable report widgets, including real-time streaming to allow you to track audience growth across all channels in one place.

Our AI solutions support rich features language interpretation to allow you to track sentiment and mentions enabling you to visualize and understand what is driving your growth.

Blog Management

Flockrush syncs content with your WordPress blogs via Jetpack or with a built-in Flockrush blog

Synchronizing your content across social pages and blogs with high volumes of content is time-consuming.

With Flockrush we integrate with Wordpress.com and Wordpress.org blogs via the Jetpack plugin and with every plan also offer you our own fully featured blog solution for frictionless publishing.

Built on the latest JAMstack.org architecture to make sure you gain fast page load speeds and greater security, our blog solution gives you huge benefits, without any of the hassles of hosting.
