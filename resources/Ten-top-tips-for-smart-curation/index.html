Ten top tips for smart curation

"A curator is an information chemist. He or she mixes atoms together in a way to build an info-molecule. Then adds value to that molecule"
- Robert Scoble

Tips for curating with the best:

Work with a subject that you are interested in
Curation needs creativity, which will be easier if you are interested in why you're posting.
Develop a consistent style
Growing and retaining followers needs consistency, develop a style that works and use it.
Add value to your posts
Curation needs to add commentary, questions, observations to posts that will increase your fans' engagement audiences need entertaining.
Have an opinion
When sharing content add your personality to it and give a view on what you think is great about it
Ask for a response
With good quality content a simple request for interaction can trigger a wave of engagement, so don't hold back.
Make it sharable
Package your content to make it as sharable as possible, picking great images that will get noticed, adding entertaining quotes, and give it broad appeal.
Know your audience
Take time to look at your audience demographics and interests and see who engages with your content. Look for factors that can help you curate even better.
Respond to your audience
Just because you haven't created what you've shared doesn't stop you engaging with audience comments, and here you can really show your personality and help build your page brand
Look at the numbers
There's no end to the amount of analytics data you have to pour over, but understand the numbers and you are more likely to succeed because you will see what works, and what doesn't.
Recycle and reuse
Save your best content and re-use it, remembering most of your audience are unlikely to see your organic content in their feeds. By re-publishing it you can continue to grow engagement significantly

Becoming a great curator gives you the opportunity to grow audiences without the high cost of creating your own brand content.
