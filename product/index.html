UGC Rights Management

Get rights to user-generated content the easy way, with automated workflows to queue publishing on approval

For peace of mind, Flockrush features an automated single-click process to handle approval rights for user-generated content.

With our smart workflow solution, any UGC requiring owner approval can be queued for posting subject to receiving approval.

Once owners give approval using our single-click process, content is automatically scheduled, leaving an electronic paper trail you can use anytime you may need it.
