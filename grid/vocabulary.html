The Vocabulary of the Future

The legacy tech industry uses words to hide their limitations. We use words to define our architecture.
To understand the Grid, you must first unlearn the "Copilot."

AI-Assisted
The Trap

A stateless chatbot bolted onto a legacy database. It waits for you to click "Approve." It is a faster horse.

Autonomous-First
The Standard

The Intelligence IS the OS. Agents are stateful, persistent processes. They don't suggest; they execute the mission.

Human-in-the-Loop
The Bottleneck

The human is a process worker. A fallible operator who slows down the machine to human speed. A vulnerability.

Human-ON-the-Loop
The Governor

The human is a Legislator. You set the "Policy Mesh" (Constitution), and the swarm executes within those bounds.

Model Governance
The Check-Engine Light

A dashboard that tells you AFTER a model has failed. Reactive. Passive. Too late.

Mission Governance
The Self-Healing Engine

Active supervision. If an agent fails, the Grid autonomously replans, resets, and retries. The mission never stops.
