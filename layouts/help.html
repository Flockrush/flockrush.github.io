The #1 Targeted Social Audience Building App: Flockrush

Flockrush is a feature rich strategy-driven app for marketing teams to efficiently build big, targeted social audiences.

System Requirements

Your Flockrush App is built to provide the best experience under the following technical conditions:

  • Google Chrome Browser Version 48.0.25xx or higher
  • Minimum 13" Monitor, preferably 15" or higher
  • Mac, but we have nothing against PC's :)

What is planned in the future versions?

Flockrush is an open and highly functionally scalable marketing communication and predictive analytics technology, which makes new feature add-ons to have a faster turn around time. We are always looking for feedback so we can make this the best platform for you!

Short term: Expect to see new versions rolling with some cool features. We have an extensive product roadmap, so sit back and enjoy the journey.

Long term future we will provide support for smaller screen footprints.
